Arduino Project


Arduino is an open source hardware and software project, created with a simple aim in mind, to be as simple as possible.

Arduino is not some hardware you should be afraid of.

Arduino comes in a variety of flavors and sizes. It is used by artists, hackers, hobbyists, and professionals to easily design, prototype and experiment with electronics.

Laser Cutter Project


Laser cutting is a technology that uses a laser to cut materials, and is typically used for industrial manufacturing applications, but is also starting to be used by schools, small businesses, and hobbyists.

Laser cutting works by directing the output of a high-power laser most commonly through optics.
